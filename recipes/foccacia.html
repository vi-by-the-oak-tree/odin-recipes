Foccacia

Description

This recipe is for a simple, no-knead foccacia. It is a transcript of instructions from Justine Doiron's video on the topic, that can't be found on her website.

With a few easy steps and some time you get bouncy, bubbly and crusty focaccia!

Ingredients

To make focaccia you will need:

  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 600mL water
  • 650g bread flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • olive oil
  • optionally: rosemary, and pitted olives to put on top
